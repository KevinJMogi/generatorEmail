Via ATM BCA

1. Pilih Menu "Transaksi Lainnya"
2. Pilih Menu "Transfer"
3. Pilih Menu "Ke Rek BCA Virtual Account"
4. Masukkan Kode Bayar Virtual Account Anda lalu pilih Benar.
5. Periksa kembali Kode Bayar (virtual account) serta Nominal Pembayaran Anda, jika sudah sesuai pilih "YA"
6. Ikuti langkah selanjutnya sampai transaksi selesai.

Via Internet Banking BCA

1. Pilih menu "Transfer to BCA Virtual Account"
2. Masukkan Kode Bayar Virtual Account Anda kemudian pilih Lanjutkan
3. Periksa kembali Nomor Virtual Account dan Nominal Pembayaran Anda, jika sudah sesuai masukkan PIN key BCA APPLI 1
4. Ikuti langkah selanjutnya sampai transaksi selesai.

Via m-BCA (BCA Mobile)

1. Pilih menu m-Transfer
2. Pilih menu Transfer BCA Virtual Account
3. Masukkan Kode Bayar BCA Virtual Account Anda kemudian pilih OK
4. Tampil konfirmasi Kode Bayar BCA Virtual Account dan rekening pendebetan, lalu Kirim
5. Periksa kembali Kode Bayar Virtual Account serta Nominal Pembayaran Anda, jika sudah sesuai pilih YA
6. Ikuti langkah selanjutnya sampai transaksi selesai.

Thank you for choosing elevenia as your online shopping partner.
Please complete your payment with Bank Transfer BCA (Virtual Account) with the following steps :

Via ATM BCA

1. Choose "Transaksi Lainnya" menu
2. Choose "Transfer" menu
3. Choose "ke rek BCA Virtual Account" menu
4. Input Your Virtual Account Number, then choose YA
5. Please check Your Virtual Account Number and Amount of Payment, if correct choose "YA"
6. Follow next step until finish

Via Internet Banking BCA

1. Choose "Transfer to BCA Virtual Account"
2. Input Your Virtual Account Number on ”Virtual Account Number” column then click ”Lanjutkan”
3. Double check Your Virtual Account Number and Payment Amount, if correct, input PIN Key BCA APPLI 1
4. Follow next step until finish

Via m-BCA (BCA Mobile)

1. Choose m-Transfer menu
2. choose Transfer BCA Virtual Account menu
3. Input Your Virtual Account Number, then choose OK
4. Confirmation about Virtual Account Number and debit account information will appear, then choose Kirim
5. Please recheck Your Virtual Account Number and Amount of Payment, if correct choose YA
6. Follow next step until finish
